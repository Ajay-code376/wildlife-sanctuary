BAT-EARED FOXES

The bat-eared fox is another Kenyan animal that belong to the dog family.Its large ears
are its defining characteristics.It also feeds on insects like termites,lives alone or in small
groups and has six more teeth than other foxes.

The bat-eared fox can be found in savannnahs and scrublandsand grassy plains.It lives
in countries including Kenya,Tanzania,South africa,Angola and Zimbabwe.The
IUCN lists the bat-eared fox as least concern and as having a stable populaion,though
they are threatenedby hunters,disease and drought.
